Our Approach

Driven by Purpose

Three pillars of our approach to eliminating food waste across Telangana.

Source Reduction

We help businesses track and minimize waste using smart analytics before it even happens.

Redistribution

Fast, efficient connection between restaurants, households, and local shelters — under 30 mins.

Sustainable Valorisation

Unavoidable waste is converted into compost or renewable energy through our partner network.

The Process

How FWMS Works

Connecting surplus food to empty plates in four simple steps.

1

Donor Submits

Restaurant or household posts surplus food with photo and location via our AI-powered form.

2

AI Verifies

Our computer vision model confirms the food photo is genuine before alerting volunteers.

3

Volunteer Claims

A nearby volunteer claims the pickup and navigates directly to the donor's location.

4

Food Delivered

Surplus food is safely delivered to shelters, ensuring zero waste and zero hunger.

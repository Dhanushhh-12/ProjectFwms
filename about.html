What We Stand For

Our North Star

The values and vision that drive every decision we make.

Our Vision

To create a world where no edible food is wasted — ensuring every person has access to nutritious meals through a seamless, technology-driven redistribution network.

Our Mission

To empower businesses and individuals to minimize food waste by providing an efficient platform that connects surplus food with those in need, fostering a sustainable and compassionate community.

The Process

How FoodSeva Works

Four simple steps connecting surplus food to empty plates.

1

Donor Submits Food

Restaurants or events log surplus food on our platform with an AI-verified photo, location pin, and expiry time.

2

AI Validates and Alerts

Our COCO-SSD vision model confirms the food image is genuine, then instantly notifies available volunteers in the area.

3

Volunteer Picks Up

A certified volunteer claims the pickup and navigates directly to the donor's GPS-pinned location via Google Maps.

4

Delivered to Shelter

Food is safely transported and delivered to partner shelters, ensuring zero waste and zero hunger in Telangana.

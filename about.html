The FWMS Core

A systematic, technology-driven approach to zero-waste.

Food Recovery Hierarchy

FWMS focuses on a systematic approach to maximize the utility of edible food and minimize environmental harm.

1. Source Reduction

Preventing waste at the start through accurate demand forecasting and inventory tracking for businesses and households.

2. Redistribution

Bridging the gap by connecting donors (restaurants, events, individuals) with recipients like NGOs and shelters in real-time.

3. Valorisation

Converting unavoidable waste into useful byproducts like animal feed, premium compost, or renewable energy (Biogas).
